Innovation Timeline

The path to autonomous care: From mechanical prototype to verified intelligence.

May 2024

The Birth of IINTS

Launch of the first functional prototype based on Raspberry Pi Pico. Focused on demystifying the fundamental mechanics of insulin delivery.

July - Dec 2024

Mechanical Precision

Introduction of the user interface and implementation of microstepping. Increased dosage accuracy to a clinically relevant level.

April - May 2025

National Recognition

Awarded "Most Technically Complex Project" at Coolest Projects Belgium and named "Student in the Spotlight".

July - Oct 2025

International Validation

Presented at CIONET and HackYeah (Krakow).

Jan 2026

Transition to Intelligence

Shift from hardware-focused V1 to the AI-driven research platform: IINTS-AF.

March 27, 2026

Launch of IINTS-AF

Official release of the Adaptive Framework. An open-source gateway for dependent validation of AI-driven diabetes care via Digital Twin technology.
